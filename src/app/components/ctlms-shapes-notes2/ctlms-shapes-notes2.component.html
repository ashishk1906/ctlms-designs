Notes

3 - Dimensional Shapes

3 - Dimensional object will have length, breadth, and height. In 3D shapes, some of the edges are hidden. In 3D shapes, only outer dimensions can be explained (Ex: Cylinder, cube, cuboid, and cone).

Cylinder

A cylinder is a three-dimensional solid figure that has two identical circular bases joined by a curved surface at a particular distance from the center, which is the height of the cylinder.

Solid Cylinder and Hollow Cylinder

When 15 circular plates are placed one above the other, we get a solid cylinder.

When 15 hula hoops are placed one above the other, we get a hollow cylinder.

TANGRAM

A traditional Chinese puzzle or riddle shapes made of a square divided into seven pieces (one parallelogram, one square, and five triangles) that can be arranged to match particular designs. These shapes are called “Tangrams”.
