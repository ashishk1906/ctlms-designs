Lesson Plan

Gist Of The Lesson

Different shapes and designs

Straight and curved edges

Corners and edges of different objects

Shapes of Animals and Birds with the help of diagram (Tangrams)

By using different shapes, designs of rugs, sarees, shawls, floor patterns, and tiling patterns.

Identify 2-D shapes formed by paper folding and paper cuttings.

Name and compare various 2-D shapes by counting their sides, corners, and diagonals.

Construct some simple 3-D objects using a ruler/free hand.

By piling coins and bangles, construct a solid cylinder and a hollow cylinder respectively and also establish a relation between 2-D and 3-D objects.

Arrange the tiles of a familiar shape in a given region.

Distinguish between similar shaped tiles and dissimilar shaped tiles.

Read a simple map.
